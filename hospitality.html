HOSPITALITY

Hospitality

ACCOMODATIONS:
All the participants coming to the summit will be provided accommodations in the bhawans(Hostels) after the successful completion of accreditation process by the organisers. The accreditation process includes verification of individuals and then afterwards you will be provided accommodation details by the team. One of our team members will volunteer with you to help you to accommodate and be familiar with the college. You will also be provided college map for further help and make your stay here easy and comfortable. Accommodation will be available from 12pm on 8th February till 10am on 11th February.
CATERING:
At the Summit, three meals a day will be provided free of charge. Meals will be served to you in the mess of your respective bhawans(Hostels) of accommodation. Please note that accreditation badge will be required to access the catering services. At the Summit, there will also be additional food stands, restaurant areas and vending machines selling food which can be paid for using cash or bank cards.
Charges:
The charges without accommodation are Rs.400 and with accommodation are Rs.800 per head for all 3 days and the Cultural night is free for the people taking accommodation. This charge will be for a maximum stay of 3 days i.e. from 12pm on 8th February till 10am on 11th February and will be the same irrespective of the duration of stay (even if vacated early). Also, please note that this fee does not include any food service. The payment has to be done through the Online Portal whose link will be given to all those allotted accommodation in the IIT Roorkee campus. Link for online payment will be shared with you once the accommodation portal is online. National Social Summit is not liable to refund the amount in case of any mistake made by the person paying the amount.
