Empowerment through Education

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.”

These powerful words from Nelson Mandela highlight the importance and significance of Education in one’s life. Education gives one the knowledge to observe the world around us and changes it into something better. Given the history of education in India, the current scenario has been charmless and future of children studying in schools seems quite bleak. The situation of government schools has been super-critical. A lot has been said, announced but everything has been backburned and laid in abeyance. Now, “Its time to Act”. With this inspiration in our minds, National Service Scheme, IIT Roorkee proudly presents National Social Summit 2019 under the theme “Empowerment Through Education”, focusing on improving the education standard in India at the grass-root level through conducting various events like inspirational guest lectures, case studies, social street plays etc. We are looking at this Summit as tiny little yet dominant step towards the improvement of Education standard, and hope that it will bring out positive and fruitful results.

“The roots of education are bitter, but the fruit is sweet.”

-Aristotle
