FAQs

How to reach?

The most preferable nearest airport is New Delhi International Airport which is about 180 kms away from Roorkee.
Roorkee is well connected to Delhi by rail and road. IIT Roorkee campus is located at a distance of 4kms from Railway station and 200 m away from Bus stand. You can hire a taxi or an e-rickshaw from outside to reach the campus.

What type of accommodation will be provided?

Accommodation will be provided to boys and girls in well secured separate residential complexes on the campus of IIT Roorkee.
This type of accommodation may be on sharing basis.

Does accommodation fee include the food facility as well?

At the Summit, three meals a day will be provided free of charge. Meals will be served to you in the mess of your respective bhawans(as per accommodation).
There will also be food courts, restaurant areas selling food which can be purchased by using cash, atm cards or e-wallets.
